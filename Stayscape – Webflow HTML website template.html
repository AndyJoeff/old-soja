What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in begins at 2:00 PM and check-out is by 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be arranged based on availability.

Is breakfast included, and what dining options are available?

Continental breakfast is included for all room bookings. We also offer a menu of local and continental dishes in our dining area every morning and a la carte options throughout the day.

Do you provide free Wi-Fi and parking?

Yes — we offer complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi throughout the property and secure free parking for all guests.

What safety and security measures are in place?

Old Soja Residence maintains 24/7 security personnel, CCTV throughout common areas, and electronic key access to guest floors. Your safety and comfort are our priority.
